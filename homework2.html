Homework 2

Subject: Both images are taken from a third‑floor view of a New York City street — with stand out yellow cars in the foreground, and a distinctive blue background. I really liked the original color scheme of the first one, but I wanted to up the saturation of the colors which naturally complimented each other. The second image is a more experimental take on the same scene, where I played with an inverted color scheme.

Creative choice: For the second version (IMG_0284.PNG), I wanted to experiment with what I think is a semi-inverted color scheme — not a simple negative. I've been playing with hues and just altering them by changing one of the colors, in my physical art. It transforms the familiar street into something surreal, almost like a dream version of the same view. While the original respects the documentary feel, the inverted one explores how color alone can redefine atmosphere. Both start from the same composition, but each tells a different emotional story.
